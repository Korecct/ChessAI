About ChessAI

Online PvP

The Online PvP gamemode allows two players to play against each other over the internet in realtime. Using a randomly generated lobby code, this gamemode allows for a simplistic way to connect two players to the game. The board is also properly oriented for the player, so their pieces will always be at the correct orientation as is expected if they were playing in person.

Player vs AI

Our player versus AI allows a user to play against different types of AIs developed for this game. We currently offer two main AIs to play against, which are our Minimax AI and our SmartyAI. Our Minimax AI is based off the minimax algorithim, a powerful algorithm that allows for intelligent gameplay algorithm, it does this by the use of weights per piece and simulating possible moves number a certain amount of turns in the future. Our smartyAI is our custom AI that is based on a simple custom algorithm. SmartyAI is designed to be a light and easy AI to engage against, perfect for beginners.
